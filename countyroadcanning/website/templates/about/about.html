About Us

Our venture sprouted from our own gardenâ€™s bounty teeming with Jalapenos and Green Tomatoes, sparked by a family tradition. Inspired by my mom's encouragement to whip up our Uncle's beloved Jalapeno Jelly and ChowChow Relish, what started as heartfelt gifts swiftly transformed into a bustling demand from friends and family, propelling us to venture into sharing our creations with a wider audience.

With a passion for crafting unique flavors, we delved into uncharted culinary territory, dreaming up recipes that promise taste sensations like no other. Our journey began at local Farmer's Markets and Festivals, where we proudly showcased our one-of-a-kind products. And for businesses seeking a taste of Texas charm, we crafted bespoke Taste of Texas Gift Sets, perfect for sending warm holiday wishes to valued clients.

Nestled in Kaufman, TX, our operations hum within a fully compliant commercial kitchen space, ensuring that every jar meets the stringent standards of the Texas Food Manufacturing License. Employing the Hot Water Bath method, our Jellies, Relishes, and Salsas come to life, brimming with the flavors of locally sourced veggies from our garden and neighboring farmers. To keep the produce flowing year-round, we've expanded our garden and built a greenhouse, ensuring a steady supply of fresh ingredients.

Among our proudest moments are the accolades from the Texas State Fair, where 14 of our Jellies clinched awards. And being chosen as a finalist by Martha Stewart and her discerning panel for the 2014 Martha Stewart American Made Awards in the food category was an affirmation of our commitment to creating something truly exceptional.

At our core, it's all about infusing every jar with dedication, top-notch ingredients, boundless imagination, and a promise of unparalleled taste.
